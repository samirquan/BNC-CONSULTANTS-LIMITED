Our Services

End-to-end cost management solutions ensuring financial success for your construction projects.

Cost Estimation and Planning

Detailed cost estimates at every stage of design, from feasibility to pre-tender. We help you set realistic budgets and plan cash flow effectively.

Bills of Quantities (BQ) Preparation

Comprehensive and accurate measurement of works. Our BQs provide a solid basis for tendering, valuation, and cost control.

Tender Documentation and Analysis

Preparation of robust tender documents and rigorous analysis of bids to ensure you select the right contractor at the right price.

Contract Administration

Managing the contractual relationship between client and contractor. We handle instructions, variations, claims, and disputes professionally.

Project Cost Control and Monitoring

Ongoing financial tracking against the budget. We provide regular reports and early warnings of potential overspends.

Feasibility Studies

Assessing the financial viability of proposed projects before you commit capital. We analyze costs vs. potential returns.

Value Engineering

Optimizing project value by analyzing function. We suggest alternative materials or methods to reduce cost without sacrificing quality.

Risk Management

Identifying, analyzing, and mitigating financial risks associated with construction projects.

Final Account Preparation

Settling the final cost of the project fairly and accurately, ensuring all variations and claims are properly adjusted.
